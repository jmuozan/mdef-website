The Essence of AI-Generated Holograms

Imagine a tool that doesn't just assist with design but also breathes life into your ideas. That's exactly what our project is: a design assistant that caters to both creatives and non-creatives. The process is simple yet powerful. Speak your thoughts into existence, and our tool will project them as a hologram, whether it be a 3D model or an image representing your concept. The magic happens through a blend of technologies, including speech-to-text, 3D modeling, animation, and holographic display formatting, all automated by our script.

From Words to Holographic Wonders

The journey from a spoken idea to a holographic projection is a fascinating one. It begins with a voice description, which is then transformed into a model or image. This is animated and rendered in Blender, and finally, formatted for a holographic display. Our script acts as a director, orchestrating various technologies to produce a holographic video from an initial spoken description.

DIY Your Personal Hologram Setup

To experience the magic firsthand, you'll need a few things: Blender for running the code, OpenAI and kittycad keys for accessing APIs, and some hardware for the artifact. The detailed instructions we've provided ensure that anyone with a bit of technical savvy and a passion for creativity can recreate this project. The essence of DIY in our project lies not just in assembling the physical components but in engaging with and understanding the technological marvel behind it.
