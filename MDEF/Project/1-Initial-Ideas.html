Where Ancient Craftsmanship Meets Modern Technology

18/05/24024

Introducing NeoCrafters an innovative initiative combining traditional craftsmanship with AI technology. This project springs from a deep appreciation for artisan skills and a commitment to preserving these arts for future generations.

The Concept

Through NeoCrafters, I aim to use machine learning and deep learning to capture the detailed movements of artisans. The result is an educational platform that offers digital mentorship, teaching age-old crafts through modern, interactive tutorials.

The First Prototype

I am thrilled to share that the first prototype of the project is now in working. This prototype uses AI to observe and learn from people as they perform different movements and it meticulously records every detail, from the positioning of hands to the subtle movements. The system then translates this data into interactive educational screen

Looking Ahead

The road ahead is filled with challenges and opportunities. As I continue to refine the AI's capabilities and expand the tools available on the platform, my commitment remains strong to ensure that the traditional crafts are not just preserved but revitalized and celebrated.
